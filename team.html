Our Core Team

Rooted in Skye & Raasay. The experience to deliver & the integrity to do it right.

Tòrr Civils & Building is led by Norman Gillies, Sandy Macaskill and Aonghas MacDonald. All grew up within 10 miles of our base on the Isle of Raasay and each brings their own unique depth of experience and specialist skills to the business.

Leadership

Norman Gillies

Managing Director

Leads all operations across Tòrr Civils & Building. Norman started on site as a labourer over 20 years ago and spent many years as a plant operator before moving into management. He has comprehensive experience in civil engineering, plant operations and project management, alongside a background in the energy and food & drink sectors that gives him a broad understanding of what different clients need and how construction fits into their wider objectives.

Qualifications

Civil Engineering & Construction Management MSc

Aonghas MacDonald

Contracts Manager

Aonghas spent many years working across the Highlands with one of Scotland's major civil engineering contractors, on energy, marine and infrastructure projects. As Contracts Manager he coordinates resources between projects, liaises with clients, and manages consents and approvals. Having had single point responsibility for overseeing dozens of geographically dispersed sites, very little phases him.

Qualifications

HND Civil Engineering · SVQ Level 4 Construction Site Manager — Contracting

Sandy Macaskill

Foreman

Sandy leads the Tòrr joinery team with extensive experience in traditional and contemporary carpentry. Equally comfortable delivering multi-unit timber framed housing, back to the wall renovations, shuttering or bespoke interior carpentry. His skills and calm manner are invaluable in delivering some complex and intricate projects with minimal fuss.

Qualifications

City & Guilds · SVQ L3 · CSCS Gold Card · SMSTS

Service 01

Project Management & Design & Build

We have long-standing relationships with architects, designers, engineers and consultants we can call on for both residential and commercial projects. We can take full responsibility for a project, from initial scope and design development through to handover — reducing pressure on clients unfamiliar with the construction process and reducing financial risk by having an experienced contractor involved from the outset.

We are equally comfortable acting as principal contractor or supporting a client's own team in a management capacity. Either way, we bring the same approach: clear communication, realistic programming, and the experience to anticipate problems before they become costly.

  • Design Coordination
  • Full Turnkey Delivery
  • Principal Contractor
  • Programme Management
  • Subcontractor Coordination
  • Cost Management
  • Consents & Approvals
  • Remote & Island Logistics

Service 02

Civil Engineering & Groundworks

Tòrr have a well resourced civil engineering capability: both in plant and equipment and experienced personnel. With decades of experience on major civil engineering projects between us, we can provide a holistic service in house. All delivered by seasoned and CPCS, CSCS or NPORS qualified plant operators and groundworkers. Our fleet of modern plant is supplemented with specialist attachments including tilt rotators, rock breakers, screener buckets and selector grabs — creating a versatile package of resources for any eventuality.

  • Footings & Foundations
  • Site Engineering & Topographical Surveying
  • Muck Shift
  • Road Construction
  • Rock Breaking & Crushing
  • Drainage
  • Hard Landscaping
  • Concrete Works
  • Retaining Structures

On-Site Hydraulic Hose Repair

We offer a mobile on-site hydraulic hose repair and assembly service across Skye & Lochalsh. When a machine goes down on site, waiting days for a part to arrive isn't an option — we carry a comprehensive stock of hose, fittings and end connections to manufacture replacement hoses to specification on the spot, getting plant back in service with minimal downtime.

  • Hose Assembly & Manufacture

  • Fishing Vessels
  • Mobile Service — Skye & Lochalsh

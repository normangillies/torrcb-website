Raasay Distillery — Na Bothain

Borodale, Isle of Raasay

Tòrr were contracted to manage the design and construction of five bespoke guest cabins to increase the overnight capacity of Raasay Distillery's guest accommodation. Working with Storey & Co and Skye Designs to develop the design, Tòrr subsequently delivered all groundworks, structure, services and landscaping on a steeply sloping, knotweed-contaminated site beside a working distillery and visitor centre. Works included cabin construction and cladding, gabion retaining structures, fabrication of steel supports and staircases, a district heating scheme, sauna & plunge pool and decking.

Inverarish Social Housing Development

Inverarish, Isle of Raasay

A collaborative project between Tòrr and Raasay Development Trust, with support from Carbon Neutral Islands, Scottish Land Fund and Scottish Government's More Homes. Tòrr purchased two derelict houses and secured grant funding through More Homes to produce building warrant drawings to modern social housing standards. This enabled Raasay Development Trust to secure CNI, Scottish Land Fund and More Homes funding to renovate and include a third house as part of the development. Works include full renovation of three terraced houses, attic conversions, underpinning to create a basement room and installation of air source heat pumps. The houses will become available for affordable rent in perpetuity when complete in late 2026.

10 Mill Place

Isle of Raasay

Tòrr originally priced up the client's own drawing pack but found the design to be prohibitively expensive to construct. We redesigned the project to remove almost half the project value, taking responsibility for all design, approvals, consents and liaising with local authorities.
